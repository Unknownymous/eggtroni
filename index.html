Eggtronic & Einova

Eggtronic (and it's recently launched Einova brand) is on a mission. To power the world! Literally. Revolutionary, eco-friendly charging tech, united with the very best in product and user design; that's who we are. Headquartered in Italy, with offices in Guangzhou, China and Long Beach, California Eggtronic's beautifully designed and energy-efficient wireless chargers, power banks, and power adapters are sold in retailers across the US and Canada and soon in Europe!
